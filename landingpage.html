WELCOME TO MY PARLOUR

Vanilla has a smooth, classic, and aromatic taste. Its light flavor makes it perfect on its own or paired with any dessert.

Chocolate is a rich and creamy flavor made from cocoa. It’s one of the most popular flavors and gives a deep, sweet taste enjoyed by all ages.

Strawberry offers a sweet and slightly tangy flavor made from fresh berries. Its pink color and fruity taste make it refreshing.

Butterscotch has a buttery, caramel-like sweetness. It’s smooth, crunchy (sometimes with nuts), and very comforting in flavor.

Black currant has a bold, fruity, slightly tangy taste. Its deep purple color and unique flavor make it stand out among berry flavors.
